KPI (Key Performance Indicator): This is a quantifiable measure of performance over time for a specific objective. This is more of the idea of the target goal for a month, the key performance is indicated that is the idea here. 

There are four different types of KPIs

Strategic: These are the more business  goals, this might be the amount of sales we want to work towards each quarter and so on.

Operational: These are focused on a tight time frame, this might be more grainular in focus, as these might be inspired by strategic KPI's such as how many of this item sold on this day.

Functional: These focus on a specific department or function within a company. So we might focus on the output of products from the manufacturing department. 

Leading: These are more of how we can target the future and aim more at the future.
